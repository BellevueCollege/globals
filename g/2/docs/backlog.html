Headings and Paragraphs

The top margins get reduced to on content blocks that immediately follow any heading level block. This affects the following tags<p> <ul> <ol>. In addition, this affects any heading followed by the next heading in the hiearchy e.g. <h2> + <h3> or <h5>+ <h6>.
